## Gaps Summary

No gaps. All five hardening items are implemented and wired:

- **Item 9 (path normalization):** `normalize_for_exclusion()` canonicalizes, lowercases, and strips trailing separators; applied to excluded list before the WalkDir loop and per-entry during traversal.
- **Item 10 (WalkDir depth guard):** `.max_depth(8).follow_links(false)` on WalkDir builder; test confirms depth-9 content is not discovered.
- **Item 12 (extended-path buffer):** `EXTENDED_MAX_PATH = 32_767` declared inline in `resolve_lnk`; MAX_PATH import removed.
- **Item 6 (bounded icon pool):** `rayon::ThreadPoolBuilder::new().num_threads(4)` pool; `icon_pool.spawn()` replaces `std::thread::spawn`; implicit join via Drop at end of `run_full_index`.
- **Item 11 (COM worker isolation):** `spawn_com_worker()` owns the COM apartment; `CoInitializeEx`/`CoUninitialize` balanced on the worker thread; `resolve_lnk` no longer calls `CoInitializeEx`; `crawl_dir` routes all .lnk resolution through the channel; `com_tx` threaded through all callers including `reindex()` Tauri command via `Arc<SyncSender>` managed state.
